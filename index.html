SCIT Trusted by millions

This Tool is the Property of Young Professionals Mentoring Initiative of Nigeria, and was conceived by the Founder/Chairman in response to the high and increasing rate of graduate unemployment in Nigeria & Africa, as well as the working Poverty amongst graduates. Students in Nigeria & Africa after Secondary or High School do not have enough information to make good decisions about their careers, with majority relying on the decisions of their Parents, Teachers and University Admission Authorities. The Tool was designed with the funding support from the German International Cooperation (GIZ) & validated by majority of the Stakeholders in the Educational sector in Nigeria, including the Ministry of Education, Nigerian University Commission & Nigerian Board for Technical Education.

ABOUT US

The Student’s Career Information Tool (SCIT) is intended to serve as a guide to Students in choosing, planning and implementing the perfect Career. The tool seek to enable the User who is believed to be someone in Secondary or Tertiary Institutions or any one who is curious in obtaining knowledge on what Career to pursue that will result in maximum reward in terms of Compensation and Satisfaction, especially African Students.....
